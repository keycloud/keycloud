About

This is the KeyCloud application developed for the software engineering class. With this application, you are able to safely store your passwords.

How to use

Dashboard

At the top right of your screen you can see three buttons, Dashboard, Settings and Logout.
Clicking on the Dashboard button takes you to the dashboard, where you are able to save passwords and their corresponding username and url to the website. Additionally, when creating a new entry, it is possible to have a password generated.
Deleting entries cannot be undone.

Settings

The page currently in the background is the Settings page of your KeyCloud account.
Here, you can retrieve the master-password, which has to be used to log into your account.
Additionally it is possible to connect your account to a second factor, i.e. a USB-safety-key, and use this second factor to log into your account.

Logout

Clicking the Logout button logs you out of your account.

Additional Information

To display this help dialog again, press the button on the settings page.
